About Us

Dewaweb was founded in 2014 to be Indonesia’s first secure, fast, and reliable cloud hosting provider because we understand that your website is a very important asset to your online business. When you choose Dewaweb, you will sleep better knowing that your website is in the good hands and we are here to protect it with our Legendary Ninja Support 24/7. Dewaweb serves more than 55,000+ customers now and is the first to receive ISO 27001 Certification, the world class gold standard for Information Security Management
